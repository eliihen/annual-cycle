Ensure adequate coverage across all teams during the summer holiday period.

## Activities

- Collect summer leave preferences from all staff
- Build a leave schedule that meets minimum staffing requirements
- Communicate the approved schedule to teams
- Arrange temporary cover or intern hiring where needed

## Outputs

- Approved summer leave calendar
- Staffing plan for July – August
