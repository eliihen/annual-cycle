Evaluate performance against the annual plan at the halfway point and update the full-year outlook.

## Activities

- Produce H1 report with financials and KPIs
- Review goal attainment against annual targets
- Present findings to the board
- Update full-year forecast based on H1 actuals

## Outputs

- H1 report to the board
- Updated full-year forecast
