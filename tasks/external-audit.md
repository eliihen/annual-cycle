Complete the statutory external audit of the annual financial statements.

## Activities

- Deliver supporting documentation package to auditors
- Respond to auditor queries and provide additional evidence
- Review draft audit report with management
- Sign and file the approved annual accounts

## Outputs

- Approved annual financial statements
- Clean audit opinion with no material findings
