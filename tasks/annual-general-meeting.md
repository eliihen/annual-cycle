Hold the ordinary AGM in accordance with company law requirements.

## Activities

- Prepare notice and agenda (at least 2 weeks before)
- Approve annual accounts and directors' report
- Resolve on dividend distribution
- Elect or re-elect board members as required

## Deadlines

- Notice must be sent at least 2 weeks before the meeting
- AGM must be held within 6 months of the financial year end
