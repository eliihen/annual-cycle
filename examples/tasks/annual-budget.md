Set and approve the financial plan for the coming calendar year.

## Activities

- Gather budget input from all departments (October)
- Consolidate proposals and run gap analysis (November)
- Present draft budget to the board (late November)
- Approve and distribute final budget (December)

## Outputs

- Board-approved budget for the next calendar year
- Department-level spending targets
