Annual performance evaluation cycle for all employees.

## Process

1. Employee self-assessment (November, week 1–2)
2. Manager reviews (November, week 3–4)
3. Calibration sessions across departments (early December)
4. One-on-one review conversations (mid December)
5. Goal-setting for the coming year (late December)
